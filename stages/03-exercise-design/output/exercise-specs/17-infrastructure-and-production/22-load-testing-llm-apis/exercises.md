2. **Expose the prompt-uniformity trap.** Create two prompt lists: one where every prompt is exactly 30 tokens, and one where prompts range from 5 to 600 tokens drawn from a realistic distribution (use snippets of varying length from a CRM export CSV or a public dataset).
